Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a red sauce pasta casserol made with layers of beef and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Red sauce
  • Cheese
  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. cook pasta
  2. Place pasta in casserol pan
  3. Add sauce
  4. Add cheese
  5. Add meat
  6. Add seasoning
  7. Add another layer of pasta
  8. Repeat
  9. Cook
